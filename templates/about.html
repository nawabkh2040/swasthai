About SwasthAI

Revolutionizing healthcare accessibility in rural India through AI technology

Our Mission

To make quality healthcare accessible to every Indian, regardless of their location or economic status, by leveraging cutting-edge AI technology.

Accessible Healthcare

Breaking down barriers to healthcare access in rural and underserved communities across India.

AI-Powered

Using advanced AI and machine learning to provide accurate, reliable medical guidance 24/7.

Community First

Building trust and empowering communities with health knowledge in their own language.

Our Story

SwasthAI was born from a simple observation: millions of Indians lack access to basic healthcare information and guidance. In rural areas, the nearest doctor can be hours away, and medical emergencies often go unaddressed due to lack of immediate guidance.

We built SwasthAI to bridge this gap. Using state-of-the-art AI technology, we provide instant medical guidance that's accessible to everyone with a smartphone. Our platform speaks the language of the people - offering support in Hindi and English mix (Hinglish) to ensure clear communication.

Since our launch, we've helped over 10,000 users across India, providing them with reliable health information, symptom checking, and guidance on when to seek professional medical help.
