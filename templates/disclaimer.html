Medical Disclaimer

Important information about using SwasthAI

CRITICAL NOTICE

SwasthAI is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. This service is an AI-powered informational tool only and should not be used for medical emergencies or as a replacement for consulting with qualified healthcare professionals.

1. Nature of Service

SwasthAI is an artificial intelligence-powered healthcare information platform designed to:

  • Provide general health education and information
  • Assist with understanding common symptoms
  • Offer guidance on when to seek professional medical care
  • Answer general health-related questions

SwasthAI does NOT:

  • Diagnose medical conditions
  • Prescribe medications or treatments
  • Provide emergency medical services
  • Replace consultations with licensed healthcare providers

2. Not Medical Advice

The information provided by SwasthAI is for educational and informational purposes only. It is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

3. No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Use of SwasthAI does not create a doctor-patient relationship. The AI assistant is not a licensed medical professional and cannot provide personalized medical advice tailored to your specific health situation.

4. Emergency Situations

In Case of Emergency

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency services immediately (e.g., 108 in India, 911 in USA) or visit the nearest emergency room.

Do not rely on SwasthAI for emergency medical situations including but not limited to:

  • Chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Severe bleeding or injuries
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Severe allergic reactions
  • Suspected poisoning or overdose
  • Stroke symptoms
  • Severe abdominal pain

5. Accuracy and Completeness

While we strive to provide accurate and up-to-date health information:

  • Medical knowledge is constantly evolving
  • AI systems may occasionally provide incomplete or incorrect information
  • Information may not be applicable to your specific situation
  • We do not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or usefulness of any information

6. Individual Medical Needs

Every person's health situation is unique. The information provided by SwasthAI is general in nature and may not be appropriate for your specific circumstances, medical history, or current medications.

7. Never Disregard Professional Medical Advice

Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read or learned from SwasthAI. If you think you may have a medical emergency, call your doctor or emergency services immediately.

8. Medication and Treatment Information

Information about medications, treatments, or procedures provided by SwasthAI is for educational purposes only. Always consult with your healthcare provider before:

  • Starting or stopping any medication

10. Regional Differences

Healthcare information, practices, and available treatments may vary by country, region, and healthcare system. Information provided may not be applicable to your specific location or healthcare context.

11. Privacy and Confidentiality

While we protect your privacy, information shared with SwasthAI is not protected by doctor-patient confidentiality. Do not share sensitive personal health information that you wish to remain completely private.

12. When to Seek Professional Help

You should consult a healthcare professional if you:

  • Have persistent or worsening symptoms
  • Are concerned about your health
  • Need a diagnosis or treatment plan
  • Are considering changes to your medications or treatment
  • Have questions about your specific medical condition
