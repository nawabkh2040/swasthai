General Questions

SwasthAI is an AI-powered healthcare assistant designed to provide general health information and guidance to people across India, especially in rural and underserved areas. We use advanced AI technology to help users understand symptoms, learn about health conditions, and know when to seek professional medical care.

Account & Login

Creating an account is easy:
  1. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up" on the homepage
  2. Enter your name, email address, and create a password
  3. Click "Sign Up"
  4. You're ready to start chatting!

Currently, password reset functionality is under development. Please contact our support team at support@swasthai.live for assistance.

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at privacy@swasthai.live with your request. We'll process your request within 7 business days.

Privacy & Security

Yes. We take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal health information with third parties without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Technical Issues

Try these steps:
  1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Try a different browser
  4. Check your internet connection
  5. If the problem persists, contact support at support@swasthai.live

SwasthAI works best on modern browsers:
  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
Make sure your browser is up to date for the best experience.
